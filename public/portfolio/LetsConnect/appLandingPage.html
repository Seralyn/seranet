Low Power Consumption

Let's Connect has been optimized to consume very little power for both iOS and Android 15. Keeping this app open will not put any significant power drain on your battery. Tested for over 1500 hours on all major smartphone brands.


Keep In Touch With Everyone You Care About

Aside from all of the connections with third party apps you can make inside of Let's Connect, the app has its own internal messenger program- designed from the ground up with your convenience in mind. Don't want to share any of your main apps with someone but still want to be able to reach them? No problem, we have you covered.


One App, All Your Connections

Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, Snapchat, TikTok, Reddit and many more! No need to open up several different apps and get several different logins ever again. Just click the box next to the apps you want to exchange info for, hit "ready to bump" and bump phones. It really is that simple.
