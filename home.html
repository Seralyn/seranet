Getting Started with Astronomy

This site was created to help answer some of the most common questions I get about backyard astronomy. Though there are many sites like this on the internet, I endeavored to create one that is clean, easy to navigate and contains only the information you might seek and nothing more. This is my contribution to the astro-community.

By clicking on the menu bar in the top left, you can find links to pages that help you choose your first telescope, whether it be for the purpose of observation, astrophotography, or both, aimed at children, or adults, or outreach programs. You can also find information on how to get started in astrophotography, how to locate objects of interest in the night sky, recommended weather apps, and the software that is available to help you in your journey, whether you use Windows, Mac, or Linux.

Clear skies, friends.
