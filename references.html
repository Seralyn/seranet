Resources

Listed below are many of the resources I have found to be of greatest use in my journey of learning about backyard astronomy. I highly recommend utilizing them to aid in your own journey as my own would not have been possible without them.

  • Cloudy Nights Forums

    • Easily the single most helpful resource I can recommend for answering your questions. The community is extremely helpful and knowledgeable. Forget Facebook forums and go here. Though the purchasing advice may be US-centric, this is easily the widest knowledge base about observational astronomy and astrophotography on the internet.

  • Stargazer's Lounge

    • A close second to the Cloudy Nights forums, the forums here serve essentially the same purpose but with slightly less activity and a large number of UK-based users.

  • AstroBackyard

    • Easily the single most helpful resource I can recommend for answering your questions. The community is extremely helpful and knowledgeable. Forget Facebook forums and go here.
