Mobile Apps

Mobile apps have come a long way in recent years and can be an excellent tool to help you familiarize yourself with the night sky, know where to point your scope or binoculars, and even interact with some of your hardware. Most prefer the versatility and control of using a proper computer for this, but the convenience of a handheld device cannot be overlooked and is sometimes the best choice.
